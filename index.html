About LexVault

The name combines "Lex" (Latin for text, rooted in lexicon) and "Vault" (a secure, organized repository) — symbolizing a protected, intelligent collection of knowledge.

Built for simplicity, LexVault helps librarians focus on what matters — connecting readers with books. No complex setup, no steep learning curve. Just a clean, fast, and reliable platform.

  • Easy to use interface
  • Works on all devices
  • No installation required
  • Free and open source
